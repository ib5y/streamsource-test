Ibrahim Syed

★ Founder and CEO of StreamSource

StreamSource was founded in 2020 by Ibrahim Syed. The app has been under devolopment for 2 years and is currently on its alpha release 2021.
The purpose of StreamSource is to provide the user the location of where the users selected movie title is streaming.

StreamSource Timeline

Date Deed Deployment
November 2020 The Spark: Searching for a movie on Google to find out what streaming provider has the movie, was wearyingly outdated. A solution must be made.
December 2020 The Study: Tireless hours of studying, mastering code and planning to create a functional app that contains a powerful search engine with high-demanding features.
March/April 2021 The Solution: StreamSource has its first alpha release on both Android and Apple platforms, and growing continuesly amongst movie fans. Search, and you will find!
